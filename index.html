Origins of football

There is evidence of organized football games in both Greece and China, that date back more the 2000 years ago, but historians have no idea how these games were played. A game that has more of a resemblance to todays football that we know and love called, "folk football" had been played since medieval times with varying rules. By "varying rules", I mean practically none.. as you could use any means to get the ball (inflated pigs bladder), towards your goal, markers or sometimes the balcony of the opponents' church, as long as it did not lead to manslaughter or murder!. Modern football originated in Britain in the 19th century, where it became a bit more professionally regulated.

Worlds most popular sport

Football is the most popular sport in the world, engaging over 4 billion fans or more then half the global population.
Football appears in the list, of the top 15 most watched sporting events in recent history, 4 times!. Beating out hugely popular sports like american football, baseball and cricket. The most watched sporting event of all time? you guessed it football. In 2022, records were broken when 5 billion people tuned in to watch the world cup in some capacity, whilst a staggering 1.5 billion watched the final between Argentina & France live. The womens world cup also did amazing numbers, ranking 4th with 2 billion viewers tuning in.
